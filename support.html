Email support
support@crosbyops.com

Crosby Ops is a workforce operations platform for Australian hospitality and retail businesses. The team behind Crosby Ops responds to every support email personally. We aim to reply within one business day.

What we can help with

  • Signing in or resetting your password.
  • Setting up a new store, employee, supplier, or integration.
  • Questions about rostering, timesheets, ordering, or end of day reconciliation.
  • Issues with Xero or Square integrations.
  • Push notification problems on iPhone or iPad.
  • Billing and subscription questions.
  • Reporting a bug or suggesting a feature.

Before you email

It helps us help you faster if your email includes:

  • Your store name and the email address you sign in with.
  • A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened.
  • A screenshot if the issue is visual.
  • The device you were on (iPhone model, web browser, etc.).

Account and data

If you need to access, correct, or delete personal data held in your Crosby Ops account, email support@crosbyops.com. We respond within 30 days. Our full privacy policy explains what we collect and why.

Response times

  • General questions: within one business day.
  • Urgent issues (cannot sign in, cannot publish payroll): same day during AEST business hours.
  • Bug reports: triaged within one business day, resolved by severity.

Hours

Support is staffed Monday to Friday, 9:00 to 17:00 ACST (Adelaide time). Emails sent outside these hours are answered the next business day.
