What it does

Four modules. One source of truth.

Rostering

Drag shifts, copy weeks, see award-correct wage cost as you build. Publishing pushes a push to every staff member.

Timesheets

Clock-in via the iPad time clock, approve weekly, export straight to Xero. Award allowances applied automatically.

Purchasing

Place orders by supplier or by product across stores. AI reads invoices, matches them to orders, posts to Xero as bills.

Leave & HR

Requests, balances, approvals. Onboarding wizard takes a new employee from offer to first shift without paper.

Built for

Owner-operators. Not enterprise.

Crosby Ops is built by a hospitality operator, for hospitality operators. Every screen earns its place because every screen is used on the floor every day. No bloat, no upsells, no twelve-step configuration wizards.
